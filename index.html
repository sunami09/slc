Welcome to the Pre-Semester Training Guide for Tutors

Welcome to the Student Learning Center (SLC) at California State University, Chico. We are excited to have you on board as we embark on a new academic year. This guide will help you navigate the pre-semester training process and prepare you for your role as a tutor.

The SLC is dedicated to fostering an inclusive and supportive learning environment. We believe in the potential of every student and are committed to helping them achieve their academic goals. Our tutors play a crucial role in this mission by providing peer-to-peer tutoring, facilitating study groups, and offering academic support in a variety of subjects.

As a tutor, you will not only help students understand course material but also equip them with the skills and confidence to become independent learners. We look forward to working with you to make a positive impact on our student community.

Detailed Subject Tutor Training Agenda - Fall 2023

The training is divided into asynchronous and in-person parts. Asynchronous learning is delivered online and done at your own pace using videos, readings, and self-paced learning modules found in blackboard. In-person training will be collaborative activities and in-depth lectures designed to prepare you to lead your tutoring sessions.

If you are not employed directly by the SLC because you work for the Business, Humanities, EECE department, etc. instead, you only need to attend training on Friday. If you are unclear on which days you should attend training, please email Catherine at cadagostini@csuchico.edu for clarification.

How to access the asynchronous Canvas Training: On Tuesday, August 8th Tutor Trainings will become available in Canvas for you to start working on. To access the training accept the invitation to join the Student Learning Center Subject Tutors Canvas page. From the Home Page, navigate to the Modules page. There are 3 sequential training modules for new tutors. Each module will unlock after you complete the one before it. Level 3 Tutors have 2 training modules. There are no asynchronous modules for returning (Level 2 tutors).

Level I (new tutors): Modules on Canvas to be completed on your own by 11:59 p.m. on Tuesday 8/15. The Tutor Cycle, Lesson Planning, SLC Policies and Procedures. Level III: Readings on Blackboard to be completed on your own by 11:59 p.m. on Monday 8/14. The Role of Learning Centers in Higher Education, Practical Applications of Contemporary Research.
